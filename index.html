Biography

My interests in computers began when I was 5. My mother would clean one of our neighbor's houses.
One day, our neighbor generously donated her old computer to my mother, who then brought it home and gave it to my sister and I.
We were excited! As soon I pressed the power button, my eyes were presented with the wonderful world of Windows 95!
After 17 years, I am in love with the world of computers and am a Computer Science major from the University of California Irvine, grauating in 2016. :D
